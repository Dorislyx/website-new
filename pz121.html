Overview

Z121 is the cost-effective High Definition and High Performance IPTV/OTT Set-Top Box. Equipped with the ARM Cortex-A9 processor and advanced software, this platform support 1080p video decoding, as well as advanced audio format, including Dolby and Dolby plus. These attractive features could fully satisfies operators/ISPs who want to launch various IPTV/OTT services.

The platform offers customization services, including remote control, package print, logo and user manual. Besides, this platform has been integrated our important partners' middleware. Operators/ISPs could choose one of our partners' solution and use their own servers to launch IPTV/OTT service.

Features:

Benefits for Operators/ISPs:

-High Performance : Leading ARM Cortex-A9 processor solution.

-Attractive features : Video decoding maximum support to 1080p. Compact design and brief interfaces.

-Seamlessly fits to existing end-to-end system : Hybroad Z121 provides an easy-to-setup and flexible means of IPTV/OTT deployment.

-Cost saving : Low cost and benefits most for Operators/ISPs.
