Overview

Z301 is an innovative and miniaturization HDMI Dongle.

Equipped with the ARM Cortex-A9 processor and advanced software, this compact device support 1080p video decoding, with Dobby and Dobby plus build-in. It's perfect for operators/ISPs who want to launch various IPTV/OTT services or make demo to user.

Highlights
  • High performance, new generation ARM Cortex-A9 processor
  • Support 1080P video decoding with a compact design.
  • More compatible, seamless fits into the existing end-to-end system, fast and flexible deployment for IPTV/ISP operators.
  • Low cost, save more for Operators/ISPs.
