Overview

X35 is the new generation Ultra High Definition and High Performance OTT Set-Top Box. Equipped with the new generation quad-core ARM Cortex-A9 processor and advanced software, this platform support 4k*2k video decoding and HEVC function, as well as advanced audio format, including Dolby and Dolby plus. Besides, this platform Voice search function by the Bluetooth RC and support mobile APP control and intendance.

Integrated the CIBN TV client, X35 offers a huge selection of TV episodes and movies. Over 200,000 TV episodes and movies, millions of songs, and over a hundred TV games. Farewell to obsolete operating experience, and begin more intelligent and free experience with X35.

Benefits for consumer:

-Better performance : New generation quad-core ARM Cortex-A9 processor solution, The performance of the machine has been advanced 4 times than previous generation Set-Top Box.

-New features : Video decoding maximum support to 4k*2k. Support HEVC. Support Voice search, Voice control function and support mobile APP control and intendance.

-Better experience for consumers : Huge selection of TV episodes and movies, millions of songs, and over a hundred TV games, Voice search, Voice control function and mobile APP control and intendance function, X35 provide a more responsive and compelling user experience.
