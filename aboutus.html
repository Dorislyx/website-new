Who we are?

Hybroad (formerly Yuxing) is a pioneer and leading IPTV/Hybrid/OTT STB provider. By the end of 2014, Hybroad has shipped out 12 million STBs to worldwide customers, such as China Telecom, China Unicom, PCCW/TVB in Hongkong, Fetch TV/Optus in Australia, Rostelecom/Megafon in Russia, KPN in Belgium, T-Home in Hungary, Etisalat in UAE, Brisanet/Life in Brazil, Liquid Telecom in Africa. As preferred partner, Hybroad also develops extensive collaboration with major companies in the ecosystem, jointly delivering end to end solution to the market. Leading edge solutions, good quality products, speed to market, effective support, price competitiveness and on time delivery are Hybroad key values recognized by customers and partners.

Hybroad is a wholly owned subsidiary of Yuxing InfoTech Investment Holdings Limited, which is listed in Hongkong stock market. Hybroad headquarter is in Beijing, with factory in Guangdong, and offices in Shenzhen and Hongkong.
