Overview

Hybroad M605T is a HD and High Performance IPTV/OTT DVB-T PVR Media Center series Set-Top Box for FetchTV.

Equipped with leading dual-core processor and three DVB-T tuners, this platform support launch IPTV/OTT service and DVB-T digital TV services.

These attractive features could fully satisfies FetchTV's demands for the second Set-Top Box.

Highlights
  • High performance, new generation Dual-core processor
  • Support 1080P video decoding, integrated three DVB-T tuners and HDD for PVR. New software platform supports Netflix and YouTube.
  • More compatible, seamless fits into the existing end-to-end system, fast and flexible deployment for IPTV/ISP operators.
  • High Security : Integrated Verimatrix high Security CAS
