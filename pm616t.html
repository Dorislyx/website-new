Overview

Hybroad M616T is the new generation Ultra High Definition and High Performance IPTV/OTT DVB-T Hybrid Set-Top Box for FetchTV. Equipped with new generation processor and four DVB-T tuners, this platform support to launch IPTV/OTT service and DVB-T digital TV services, video decoding maximum support to 4k*2k. Besides, M616T built-in a large capacity HDD, which fully satisfies users' demand for PVR service.

Equipped with the 802.11ac Wi-Fi module, this platform has faster network access and video will play more smoothly. Hybroad advanced platform and perfect technology service, makes FetchTV reach a new height in IPTV/OTT market.

Features:

Benefits for Operators/ISPs:

-High Performance : New generation B15 Apps CPU.

-Attractive features : Video decoding maximum support to 4k*2k. Integrated quad DVB-T tuners and 802.11ac Wi-Fi module. Support HEVC.

-High Security : Integrated Verimatrix Advance Security CAS.

-Seamlessly fits to existing end-to-end system : Hybroad M616T provides an easy-to-setup and flexible means of IPTV/OTT deployment.
