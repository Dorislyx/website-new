What is Our Strenght?

1. Experienced Industrial Pioneer
Hybroad is the successor of information household appliance and born for STB industry. It also possesses professional R&D technology, qualified manufacturing and producing experience for decades.
Hybroad vision is providing the solid hardware foundation and the software features to get to a unique TV proposition with least cost and effort.

2. Exclusive Supplier Delivers High Quality Offering
HYBROAD launched a various series of STBs, included IPTV, hybrid and multimedia center and so on, all of which has occupied the most markets. The characteristic products with high quality covered the categories of IPTV, OTT and hybrid STBs. HYBROAD’s product team are expertise and experienced in R&D, it aims to chase the excellent production and perfect customer’s experience.
The production of HYBROAD are popular around the world. It is remarkable that HYBROAD is the exclusive STB supplier for PCCW (HongKong) and Fetch TV (Australia). The annual shipping quantity is over 10 million. (Totally, the added up shipping quantity of IPTV STBs of Hybroad Vision was more than 10 million by the end of 2013).

3. Mature Manufacturing Technique and Well-Customized Service
HYBROAD has stable performance of hardware and powerful software R & D team who provides IPTV with end-to-end technology solutions and software application development. The biggest advantage is able to meet customized needs of different clients, such as machine appearance, packaging design, remote control, cable accessories. The hardware can be customized based on their demand as well. The software R & D team supports the private property of clients and integrates the special needs of customers.

4. broadly customers
HYBROAD has built strategic partnerships with corporations from America, Australia, Europe, HongKong, and mainland China. As a independent STB supplier, HYBROAD has established extensive and in-depth cooperation with the mainstream operators, system integrators and CA middleware solutions provider, the wide partnership power the integration of well-known middleware, conditional access / copyright management and head end solution. At present, more than 30 leading operators are using STB products worldwide. HYBROAD will expand the business partnership continually and to serve the global clients .

5. Self-owned Factory Ensures After-sale Service
Hybroad manufacturing factory is located in Guangdong Province, Zhongshan Yuxing Industrial Park (the planned area is 117 acres and the building area is about 69,000 square meters). The total yield is 300,000 units per month, which ensures the reliable production capability and quality management.The well-organized after-sale service system supported by the professional technical team that qualified with scientific and standardized service management and efficient service network, all of which provide customers with all-round technical support.
