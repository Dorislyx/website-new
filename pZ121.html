Overview

Z121 is the a High Definition with strong performance IPTV/OTT DVB-T Hybrid Set-Top Box.

With latest ARM Cortex-A9 processor build-in, Z121 can easily support 1080p video decoding. Meanwhile it supports Dobby and Dobby plus, give you a better sounding experience. These attractive features are perfect for operators/ISPs who want to launch various IPTV/OTT services.

Highlights
  • High performance, new generation ARM Cortex-A9 processor
  • Support 1080P video decoding with a compact design.
  • More compatible, seamless fits into the existing end-to-end system, fast and flexible deployment for IPTV/ISP operators.
  • Low cost, save more for Operators/ISPs.
