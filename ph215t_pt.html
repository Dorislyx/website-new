Overview

X35 is the new generation Ultra High Definition and High Performance OTT Set-Top Box. Equipped with the new generation quad-core ARM Cortex-A9 processor and advanced software,

X35 support 4k*2k video decoding and HEVC function, as well as advanced audio format, including Dolby and Dolby plus. Besides, this platform Voice search function by the Bluetooth RC and support mobile APP control and intendance.
