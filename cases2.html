PCCW Now TV

Pacific Century CyberworksLimited(PCCW), HongKong leading Telecom Operator, launched IPTV services in 2003 and now becomes one of the largest IPTV operator in the world. By the end of 2014, HYBROAD has delievered more than 1,850,000 IP/Hybird STB to PCCW.


The Growth of Now TV with Hybroad

Steady Now TV Growth Momentum:
-The media group of PCCW is a leading, fully integrated multimedia and entertainment group in Hong Kong.
-The media group operates the leading pay-TV service in Hong Kong under the nowTV brand delivering both self-produced and licensed content to its customers using advanced IPTV technology. nowTV has more than 180 channels with over 1.2 million customers of local, Asian and international programming. It is also a leading producer of Chinese language news, financial news, sports and entertainment programming in addition to music and events production which complements its wide portfolio of licensed international television content.
-At the beginning period: The new business model (such as the free STB and the new access technology etc) caused the fast increase of subscrbers.
-At the developing period: To add abundant contents (for example, the matches of English Premier League) and to provide HD contents attracting more and more subscribers.
-Recently: Dual-model STB makes the contents enrich for local programs, which enhance the customer loyalty and reduce the loss of customers.

What Hybroad brings for Fetch TV?
  • Cost-effective STB to save one-off installation and STB rental free.
  • Good quality STB to support fast and large scale roll out, low OPEX.
  • Promptly interduced innovative applications to drive ARPU growing.
  • Service center based in Hong Kong, effective local service.
