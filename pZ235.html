Overview

The Hybroad Set-Top Box Z235 is a HD STB providing a comprehensive range of features for the hospitality market.

Fully functionalities and advanced Hardware, Z235 support decoding all range of video format, as well as advanced audio format.

Highlights
  • High performance, Dual-core processor
  • 1080p video decoding. Support IR_OUT and IR_IN interface, a RJ12 interface and two RJ45 interface.
  • Perfect Hotel IPTV/OTT Set-Top Box solution, fast and flexible deployment.
  • High Security : Integrated Verimatrix Advance Security CAS
